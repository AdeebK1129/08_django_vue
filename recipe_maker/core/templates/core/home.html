Recipe Maker Instructions

  • First: Log in to Recipe Maker using your user login information.
  • Second: Use the links to "New Ingredient" to input some ingredients.
    View these ingredients in the "Ingredients List".
  • Third: Use the link to "New Recipe" to create a new recipe.
    If you are in the "Ingredients List", click "Recipes List" THEN "New Recipe".
  • Fourth: Use the link to "Recipes List" to find your created recipes.

    • Changing Ingredient Information

    • First: Go to the "Ingredients List".
    • Second: Click an ingredient name.
    • Third: Click "Update Ingredient"
    • Fourth: Update the ingredient's name information, click "Save" to save.

    Changing Recipe Information

    • First: Go to the "Recipes List".
    • Second: Click a recipe name.
    • Third: Click "Update Recipe"
    • Fourth: Update the recipes's name, ingredients, food description, or the recipe directions, and then click "Save" to save.

    Deleting an Ingredient or Recipe

    • First: Go to either the "Ingredients List" or "Recipes List"
    • Second: Click on the ingredient or recipe you wish to delete.
    • Third: Click "Delete Ingredient" or "Delete Recipe", whichever applies.
    • Fourth: Click "Confirm" to confirm deletion.
